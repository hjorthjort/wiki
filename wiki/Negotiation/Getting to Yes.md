The basic method:

1. Non-personal: Separate the People from the Problem.
2. Interest-oriented: Focus on Interests, not Positions[^1].
3. Explore options: Invent Options for mutual gain.
4. Objective: Insist on using Objective Crieteria

[^1]: A "position" is jargon for what each party say they want in a negotiation. For example, the price asked is a position when haggling.
